About me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Industrial and Data Engineering at Hongik University, Seoul, South Korea. Before joining Hongik University, I was a data scientist at Walmart Connect, the U.S. arm of Walmart's global advertising business. Also, I was a data scientist in the Artificial Intelligence team at Teikametrics, working on bidding optimization for Amazon's and Walmart's Cost Per Click Advertising Model. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Computational Research Division at the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the Department of Integrated Systems Engineering at The Ohio State University in 2019, under the supervision of Professor. Guzin Bayraksan. I received my M.S. in Industrial Engineering from Pohang University of Science and Technology, Pohang, South Korea, in 2013, under the supervision of Professor. Chi-Hyuck Jun, and B.S. in Industrial Engineering from Hanyang University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2011.
